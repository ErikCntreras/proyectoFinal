DiFIEA is a non-profit organization whose main goal is to provide advisory services for digital evidence processing, with a strong technical and legal basis.
Accessibility

The information held by the mobile device, should be accessible at any time.

Security

Implement measures to ensure that the information will not be lost.

Traceability

Knowing who, when and why, certain people accessed the information.

Confidenciality

The information should be accessible only to authorized users.

DiFEA is composed of Forensic Scientists with strong knowledge of how Digital Evidence should be handled - from discovery, collection, and transportation to analysis and presentation.

Only those who are part of a legal system can purchase our services.
